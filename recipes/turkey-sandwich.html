Turkey Sandwich

Description

A nice, delicious sandwich for any day of the year. Pair it with a crisp sprite, iced water, or caprisun and your day will be 10x better. :)

Ingredients

  • Sliced Bread of any kind
  • Turkey freshly hunted or from the store
  • Chips of any kind
  • Sliced Tomatoes from the store or homegrown
  • Lettuce from the store or homegrown
  • Barbecue sauce from the store (sweet baby rays) or homemade

Steps

  1. Grab 2 slices of bread and place them on a plate separately.
  2. Grab lettuce and place a couple pieces on one slice of bread.
  3. Grab a couple slices of tomatoes and place them on top of the lettuce.
  4. Grab some turkey and place them on top of the tomatoes.
  5. Grab a handful of chips and place them on top of the turkey.
  6. Grab the barbecue sauce and put some on top of the chips.
  7. Grab the other slice of bread and place on top of the chips.
  8. Enjoy!!
