Green Beans and Corn

Description

As simple as it looks, it is very filling and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Green Beans
  • Corn
  • Pepper
  • Salt and Garlic Powder (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix green beans and corn in a container
  2. Add pepper on top
  3. Optional - Add salt and garlic powder on top
